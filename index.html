How it works:

1. Make sure your mobile app is installed

2. Enter the custom URL scheme registered by your app

3. Click the appropriate button for your device

4. If the app is installed, it will open automatically

Note: Android has two methods:

- Intent method (recommended): Uses Android's intent system

- Direct method: Uses the standard URL scheme (may not work on all Android devices)
